1. What is this all about?
Novelty Insights is a platform for self-exploration, where your data is used not to sell you targeted ads but for you to better understand your habits and blind spots. The platform can visualize your data in ways you may not have seen or envisioned it before, and provide new contexts to what you have been doing.

2. What is with the links? Books? Music? Travel? Streaming?
These represent different domains. For books, data comes from Goodreads. For music, it can come from Spotify. For travel, it is a bit more complicated. Right now, functionality has only been built out for books, although for travel I have demonstrated a gallery of graphs. If reading books is not important to you, you might not find much from this website at the moment. Hopefully as we add more domains, we will have insights in areas you are interested in.

3. What should we learn from these visualizations?
Data is neutral. The graphs simply present truth about what a person has read, putting the books in a context that is likely novel to the reader. I believe some people will look at their graphs and find some confirmation about their patterns and interests and perhaps some surprises.
