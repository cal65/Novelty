Welcome to Novelty Insights - Streaming
Come and understand better what you have watched over the years. What series did you first watch? What genres do you watch the most? The least of? When were you the most active with your tv bingeing? What actors have you knowingly or unknowingly followed?
How does it work?
This analysis is for those with a Netflix account. You can request a data export from Netflix. On the desktop verion of https://www.netflix.com/, you can go scroll to the account page and find your user profile. Click on the arrow to the right and look for Viewing Activity. More instructions are available here. The basic export will return all the movies/episodes you have watched and the date you last watched them. The download is instantaneous.

At the header of this site, there is an Upload Data section of this site. The export you should upload is the csv file you downloaded that should be called NetflixViewingHistory.csv.
